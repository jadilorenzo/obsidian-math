Find the general solution of the differential equation $$y' = 3e^y x^2.$$
$$
\begin{align*}
\frac{dy}{dx} &= 3e^y x^2 \\
\frac{dy}{3e^y} &= x^2 \, dx \\
\int \frac{1}{e^y} \, dy &= \int 3x^2 \, dx \\
-e^{-y} &= x^3+c \\
e^{-y} &= -x^3-c \\
-y &= \log \left(-x^3-c\right) \\
y &= - \log \left(-x^3-c\right)
\end{align*}
$$
