Find the power series of the function $\sqrt{x+1}$ about $x=2$.

Let's call $f(x) = \sqrt{x+1}.$

$$
\begin{align*}
f'(x) &= \frac{1}{2 \cdot \sqrt{ x+1 }} \\
f''(x) &=  -\frac{1}{4 \cdot(x+1)^{\frac{3}{2}}}\\
f'''(x) &= \frac{\sqrt{ 3 }}{72} \\
f''''(x) &= -\frac{ 5\cdot\sqrt{ 3 }}{432}
\end{align*}
$$

$$
\begin{align*}
f(2) &= \sqrt{ 3 } \\
f'(2) &= \frac{\sqrt{ 3 }}{6} \\
f''(2) &= -\frac{\sqrt{ 3 }}{36} \\
f'''(2) &= \frac{\sqrt{ 3 }}{72} \\
f''''(2) &= -\frac{ 5\cdot\sqrt{ 3 }}{432}
\end{align*}
$$
