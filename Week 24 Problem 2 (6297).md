Let $f$ be a continuous real-valued function such that $(f'(x))^2 = f(x)f''(x)$. Suppose $f(0) = 1$ and $f^{(4)}(0) = 9$. Find all possible values of $f'(0)$. 

$$\begin{align}
(f'(x))^2 &= f(x)f''(x) \\
((f'(x))^2)' &= f(x)'f''(x)+f(x)f'''(x)  \\
2f'(x)f''(x) &= f(x)'f''(x)+f(x)f'''(x)
\end{align}$$
